Save the Date:

1st April 2020

SIGN event is free and open to the public – sign up now onEventBright

 

The SIGN Conference 2020 is coming to Solent University in Southampton on 1st April. The focus of this year's conference is on entering the games industry and finding success, as a tiny indie or major studio.

 

Registration, lunch and networking from 12pm, keynote to begin at 1pm.

 

The keynote speakers will be Andrew and Philip Oliver from Game Dragons. The Oliver brothers are long-time fixtures of the UK games industry, having launched their first game in 1983 and ran award-winning Blitz Games Studios for 23 years. Their latest endeavour is Game Dragons, with the aim of sharing the knowledge gained from years of games development experience.

 

Other speakers and details will be announced in due course.

Overview

 

SIGN is a not-for-profit network, established to represent and support independent video games developers and facilitate the growth of the independent video games development sector in the Southern region of the UK. The network is focused primarily on providing support in areas associated with business aspects of the video games industry, for example, in areas such as funding, tax, marketing and law. The support will be provided through a series of events, web-based resources and through facilitating connections between members of the network and professionals within, and complementary to, the video games industry. Our members include independent games developers, university academics, researchers, students and technology businesses.
